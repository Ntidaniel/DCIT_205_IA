Frequently Asked Questions

  • What undergraduate programs does the Computer Science Department offer?

    The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana offers a range of undergraduate programs, including Bachelor of Science (BSc) degrees in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and Software Engineering. Each program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in theoretical and practical aspects of computing.

  • How can I apply for admission to the Computer Science program?

    The admission process for the Computer Science program involves submitting an online application through the University of Ghana's admissions portal. Detailed information about admission requirements, deadlines, and procedures can be found on the official university website

  • Who are the faculty members in the Computer Science Department?

    Our department boasts a diverse and accomplished faculty with expertise in various areas of computer science, including artificial intelligence, data science, software engineering, and more. Explore the faculty profiles on our website to learn more about their research interests and contributions to the field.

  • Are there opportunities for research and internships?

    Yes, the Computer Science Department encourages and supports research initiatives and internships. Students have the chance to engage in cutting-edge research projects and gain practical experience through internships with industry partners. These opportunities contribute to a well-rounded educational experience.
