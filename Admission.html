Graduates gain hands-on experience in solving complex scientific problems, covering topics such as Advanced Databases, Data Structures and Algorithms, and Networks.

  • Phd Programme

    Our Ph.D. program is a full-time 4-year research initiative that demands a high level of commitment. Students are required to reside near the University and actively engage in the program. With guidance from the postgraduate committee, Ph.D. students customize their study program, focusing on a major specialization and at least two minor areas. Beyond coursework, regular seminars provide a platform for students and faculty to review and critique research. Students are encouraged to join one research lab and actively participate in group research activities. The department's research areas encompass Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cybersecurity.
